Inelastic neutron scattering

Magnetic scattering

  • General neutron cross section on the Bravais lattice:

$$ \frac{d^2\sigma}{d\Omega dE} = C \cdot F^2(\mathbf{K}) \sum_{\alpha , \beta}(\delta_{\alpha , \beta} - \hat{k}_\alpha\hat{k}_\beta ) (gSg^T)^{\alpha , \beta} (\mathbf{k}, \omega) $$
  • Dynamic spin correlation function:

$$ S^{\alpha \beta}(\mathbf{k},\omega) = \frac{1}{2\pi\hbar}\int dt e^{-i \omega t} \left< S^\alpha (\mathbf{k}, 0) S^\beta (-\mathbf{k}, t) \right> $$
  • General neutron cross section on a non-Bravais lattice:

$$\begin{align} \frac{d^2\sigma}{d\Omega dE} = & C \sum_{\alpha , \beta} \left( \delta_{\alpha , \beta} - \hat{k}_\alpha\hat{k}_\beta \right) \int dt e^{-i\omega t} \\ & \sum_{i,j} e^{i \mathbf{k}(\mathbf{r}_i - \mathbf{r}_j)} \left( g_i \left< \mathbf{S}_i (\mathbf{k}, 0) \mathbf{S}_j^T(-\mathbf{k}, t) \right> g_j \right)^{\alpha \beta} F_i (\mathbf{k}) F_j(\mathbf{k}) \end{align}$$
